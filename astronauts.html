Astronauts

Cool people who went into space.

Neil Armstrong

Neil Armstrong was an American astronaut and the first person to walk on the Moon. He was the commander of the Apollo 11 mission in 1969.

Yuri Gagarin

Yuri Gagarin was a Soviet pilot and cosmonaut who became the first human to journey into outer space in 1961.

Chris Hadfield

Chris Hadfield is a retired Canadian astronaut who was the first Canadian to walk in space. He is known for making videos about life on the ISS.
