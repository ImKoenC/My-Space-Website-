Our Planets

Meet all the planets in our Solar System, including extras like Pluto!

Explore

00. The Sun

This is the star of our solar system. It's so big that about 1.3 million earths could fit inside it.

01. Mercury

This is the smallest planet in our solar ssytem. A year on Mercury is only 88 Earth days.

02. Venus

The hottest planet in our solar system, and it rotates in the opposite direction to most planets.

03. Earth

Our home planet! The only known planet to support life.

04. Mars

The Red Planet. Home to Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the solar system.

05. Jupiter

This is the largest planet in our solar system. Its Great Red Spot is a storm wider than Earth.

06. Saturn

This famous for its spectacular ring system made of ice and rock.

07. Uranus

A giant ice giant that orbits on its side. And it's actually the coldest planet in the solar system!

08. Neptune

The furthest planet in our solar system. It takes 165 Earth years to orbit the Sun.

09. Pluto

Once the ninth planet, now it our favorite dwarf planet!
